## Relationship Guide

### Michael Doe & Emily Doe

- **Relationship to Parents**: Both are children of John & Jane Doe

## Relationship Types Legend

- **═════** = Married/Spouse
- **|** = Parent-Child Relationship
- **─** = Sibling Relationship

## How to Read the Tree

1. Start from the top (oldest generation)
2. Follow the lines downward to see descendants
3. Marriage relationships are shown with horizontal lines
4. Vertical lines connect parents to children
